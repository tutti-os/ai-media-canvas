# Code of Conduct

AI Media Canvas is an open-source project for people building and exploring local-first AI media workflows.

We want the community to be useful, welcoming, and safe for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when your words or actions cause harm.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the work.
- Give clear, actionable feedback when reviewing issues or pull requests.
- Respect people's time, privacy, and boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Discriminatory comments related to identity, background, or ability.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, pull requests, or maintainer work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project or community.

## Reporting

If you need to report a concern, contact the project maintainers privately through the repository owner's preferred private contact channel.

Please include relevant context, links, screenshots, or logs when safe to share. Do not include private data or secrets.
